Japanese Chicken Cutlet

Description: A tenderized chicken fillet coated in crispy panko breadcrumbs and deep-fried to a golden brown.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thigh fillets (skinless & about 150g each)
  • salt and pepper
  • 30g flour
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil to deep fry
  • tonkatsu sauce

Prep:

  1. In three seperate bowls or plates place beaten egg, flour, and panko
  2. If chicken thickness uneven cut into thickeness without cuting all the way thru and open. season chicken with salt and pepper
  3. for each fillet first coat with flour, shake excess off, then coat in egg. Allow excess to drip off, then cover in panko. repeat for rest

Cook:

  1. Heat oil in a deep-frying pan to 350F (enough oil to just about submerge chicken)
  2. Gently place fillet into the oil. Can fry multiple at a time but dont over crowd pan
  3. Fry for about 3 min or until the bottom side is browned. Flip and cook for another 3 min.
  4. Transfer onto a tray lined paper towels to drain excess oil. Rest for 5 min.
  5. After cooking all cutlets cut into strips and serve with tonkatsu sauce.
